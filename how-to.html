Use Pinterest to Share Your Mascot Vision

Want to make it super easy to show us what you have in mind? Create a Pinterest board with photos, colors, and character traits! It's a great way to communicate your ideas visually.

  1. Create a Pinterest board with the title “My Mascot Inspiration”.
  2. Pin images that capture your mascot's look, vibe, or story. You can include animals, colors, clothing, or personality traits.
  3. Copy the board link and paste it into the request form.

Need an example? Check out Nadine's sample board showing what a good inspiration board looks like.
