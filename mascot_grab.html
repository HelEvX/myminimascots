Choose Your Tutorial Style

Option 1: Mascot Grab Video Tutorial

Perfect for mascots inspired by TV shows, movies, video games, or YouTube characters. You'll edit this yourself using free tools.

What You'll Need

  • A desktop or TV screen
  • A smartphone
  • Your plush mascot
  • A video clip where your character inspiration is clearly visible (preferably in a scene where they don't move too much)
  • A well-lit room
  • Optional: Dress up however you feel comfortable!

Recording Steps

1. First Recording: The Grab

  1. Hold your smartphone in one hand so you can see the screen clearly
  2. Position yourself about an arm's length away from your TV/monitor
  3. Keep your mascot nearby but out of frame
  4. Set your video clip to play in fullscreen mode
  5. Start recording on your phone and then play the clip
  6. When your character appears, pretend to grab them with your free hand
  7. Make a mental note of exactly when you grabbed (e.g., what dialogue was happening)

2. Second Recording: The Magic Transformation

  1. Play the clip again while still recording
  2. This time, hold your mascot near the screen with the same free hand
  3. Position the mascot to approximately cover where the character appears
  4. When you reach the exact moment you made your grabbing motion before, pull the mascot toward you as if you just grabbed it from the screen

3. Final Recording: The Reveal

  1. Stop recording
  2. Switch to selfie mode on your camera
  3. Hold your mascot close to your face
  4. Record yourself hugging or interacting with your mascot for a few seconds

Editing Steps

  1. Import all your footage to your preferred editing software
  2. Cut the footage into three segments:
    • Beginning to the moment your fingers close on the first grab
    • Holding the mascot to the screen and pulling it toward you
    • The selfie hug finale
  3. Join these clips together seamlessly

Audio Tips

  • To prevent audible cuts, let the sound from the first video continue playing throughout
  • Advanced option: Layer the original video sound underneath all clips

Option 2: Mascot Catch (We Edit For You!)

A simpler option that works for ANY mascot! Nadine will create a video where she "drops" your mascot and you catch it. Perfect if you prefer not to edit videos yourself.

What You'll Need

  • Your plush mascot
  • A smartphone
  • A well-lit space with a neutral background (if possible)

Simple Recording Steps

  1. Find a spot with good lighting and enough space to move your arms
  2. Hold your smartphone with one hand (or set it up on a stable surface)
  3. Start recording a video
  4. Look up slightly as if watching for something falling from above
  5. Throw your mascot up in the air (just out of frame)
  6. Catch your mascot with both hands
  7. Bring your mascot close and give it a hug or smile at the camera
  8. End the recording

Pro tip: It might take a few tries to get the timing right - that's completely normal!
